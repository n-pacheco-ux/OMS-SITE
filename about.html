ABOUT US

WE ARE WHERE YOU NEED US

Offshores Marine Service Lda (OMS) is an Angolan Shipping Company established in 2008 by a team of industry experts with extensive experience in maritime operations and a vision to transform shipping services in the region.

EXPERTISE IN MARITIME SERVICES

As a leading Maritime Service Provider in Angola, we offer proven expertise delivered through a skilled and dedicated team. Part of a diversified conglomerate with Ship Agency as its core activity, OMS is a one-stop shop for quality shipping, maritime and logistics services.

Our expanding portfolio includes Husbandry Services, Offshore supply Services, Freight Forwarding, Project Cargo Handling, Chartering and Broking, and Logistics & Warehousing Services.

We are passionate about providing operational quality and expertise to our ever-increasing worldwide customer base. With a global reach, we are continuously expanding our network of key office locations, and today, we serve from all Angolan ports and from a representative office in the United States.

OUR VISION

To be a reference company in the oil sector in Angola, following the best industry practices.

OUR MISSION

To offer support services to all activities in the Oil and Gas sector chain, adhering to the required quality standards, creating value for our clients, stakeholders, and promoting the social development of employees and the communities.
